About Us

V Tech is a Flagship of VSM Infotech which is a Government of Rajasthan registered & ISO 9001:2008 & IAO America certified IT Training, Skill Development and Placement Company established in the year 2001. Since the year 2001 till 2015 the company was associated with IT Training giants like Tata-CMC & HCL Learning Ltd. From the year 2015 the company has started its operations under its own brand TECHNOGLOBE which is now the most Renowned brand in IT Education in Rajasthan. Technoglobe has been awarded more than 15 times for its quality education & Placements by Government & Non government organizations. In our last 22 years journey Technoglobe has trained & placed more than 30 Thousand students. Technoglobe is Authorized Partner of HPE (Hewlett Packard Enterprise), MICROSOFT, IBM-Cognitel, ADOBE, AUTODESK & TALLY Learning.It is also recommended by Rajasthan Technical University (RTU) for Internships & trainings. Technoglobe is authorised by Ministry of MSME for Skill Development
